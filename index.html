General Information:

Ethan "Ethan" Arnold (born March 2, 2000) is an American player who is currently playing for NRG. He is a former Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player known for his time in NRG Esports and Evil Geniuses where he currently plays VALORANT.

He was previously known for playing the popular FPS Tact shooter CSGO who retired after most notably playing for the NRG-EG core before retiring in February 2021 after being acquired by 100 Thieves' VALORANT division.

Some of his most notable qualities as a player are that he:

  1. Never baits his teammates to up his own stats.
  2. Tries to support his team with the best utility as possible.
  3. Is a great team player that always plays for the win.

His highest Achievements to date in CS:GO:

  • ESL One: Cologne 2020 NA - 1st
  • BLAST Premier Spring 2020 American Finals - 1st
  • Starseries x I-League S8 - 1st
  • Many more major & minor championships his team placed well in.
Some of his highest Achievements in VALORANT:
  • Americas Regional League 2023 - 3rd place
  • Masters Tokyo International 2023 - 2nd place
  • VALORANT World Champions Tour 2023 - 1st place
  • Other offseason and minor tournaments he has played in.
